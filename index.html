About me.

Hi again! I am Shishir, a 28 year old UI/UX Designer, living in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Ever since I was a child I always love creativity and adventure. From building legos to playing video games, I was always trying to create new things and explore the world of imagination.

Most of that is still true today. I always enjoy creating something new, which has led me to UI/UX design. My work tends to be graphic based. Making use of the relationships between 2-dimensional shapes, color theory, and typography. My influence comes from my childhood. Experiences from video games and different times during my life help me to brainstorm new ideas and subject matter. Another main source of my influence is the people I've known, the places I have been, and the trials I have been through.
